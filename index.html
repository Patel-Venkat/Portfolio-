Hi, I’m Patel Venkat

Photographer • Designer • Visual Storyteller

I’m Patel Venkat, a creator deeply passionate about editing, photography, and design. What started as a love for creativity slowly shaped into my own venture — Manve.studios, with the vision “Design Your Names.”

Through this brand, I’ve explored a wide spectrum of artistic expressions: wall paintings, mandala art, digital portraits, caricatures, sculptures, custom invitations, video editing, photography, and personalized frames. Every project I take on is more than just design — it’s about creating something meaningful, unique, and memorable.

For me, creativity isn’t just a skill, it’s a language — a way to tell stories, capture emotions, and design timeless identities. With every stroke, frame, and idea, I aim to inspire and leave behind a touch of artistry that reflects individuality.

As I continue to grow, my mission is to make Manve.studios not just a brand, but a name remembered for creativity, meaning, and lasting impact.

MANVE.studios

MANVE.studios was founded with a simple vision – to create designs and artworks that are not just visually beautiful but also deeply personal and meaningful. Our slogan, “Design Your Names” reflects our belief that every creation should carry its own story, emotion, and identity.


At Manve Studios we bring together art, design, and innovation to craft unique experiences. From wall paintings, mandala art, caricatures, and digital portraits to sculptures, hand castings, invitations, video/photo editing, and personalized frames, every project is made with perfection and passion. We don’t just look at the outcome as a product, but as a piece of happiness for the person receiving it.
